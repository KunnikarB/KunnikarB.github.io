Sanremo, often called the "City of Flowers," is a stunning coastal town in Italy known for its Mediterranean charm, elegant villas, and rich history.

Here are some of the best places to visit in Sanremo:

  • Old Town (La Pigna): Explore the narrow streets and historic buildings of Sanremo‚Äôs old town, La Pigna. Wander through the labyrinth of cobblestone streets, arches, and small squares of Sanremo's medieval heart. It offers breathtaking views and a glimpse into the city's history.
  • Villa Nobel: Visit the former home of Alfred Nobel, this villa is now a museum dedicated to his life and work. Now a museum and botanical garden. Explore the lush gardens and learn about the Nobel Prize history.
  • Sanremo Casino: Experience the glamour of the Sanremo Casino, one of the oldest casinos in Italy.
  • Riviera dei Fiori: Bike along the scenic Riviera dei Fiori and enjoy the stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.
  • Bussana Vecchia: Discover the artistic village of Bussana Vecchia, known for its bohemian atmosphere and creative community. Just outside Sanremo, this charming artist village is a must-see. It's a restored medieval town destroyed by an earthquake in the 19th century and later revived by artists.
  • Porto Vecchio (Old Port): Stroll along the Old Port and enjoy the picturesque views of the harbor and the Ligurian Sea where you can enjoy fresh seafood and admire colorful fishing boats.
  • Madonna della Costa Sanctuary: Visit the Madonna della Costa Sanctuary, a beautiful church perched on a hill overlooking the city for panoramic views.
  • Giardini Regina Elena: Relax in the peaceful gardens of the Giardini Regina Elena, a lovely park with stunning sea views.
  • Promenade and Beaches: Enjoy the scenic Lungomare delle Nazioni, a promenade lined with palm trees, cafes, and access to sandy beaches.
  • Santuario di Nostra Signora della Costa: This sanctuary is a spiritual and architectural gem, offering stunning views over Sanremo.
  • Sanremo Festival: If you're lucky enough to visit during the Sanremo Festival, you'll experience the excitement of Italy's most famous music festival.
  • Sanremo Market: Explore the local markets in Sanremo, where you can find flowers, local crafts, fresh produce, seafood, and local delicacies. Shop for Ligurian specialties like pesto and olive oil.
